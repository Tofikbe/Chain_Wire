GAIB x KiteAI – Compute + Yield Layer

GAIB (Global Artificial Intelligence Blockchain) is stepping into a crucial role in shaping the future of compute and yield layers inside KiteAI. This partnership aims to enhance the scalability, efficiency, and decentralization of AI-powered applications.

With GAIB’s compute infrastructure, KiteAI agents will have real-time access to processing power, enabling them to handle complex tasks at scale. Meanwhile, the yield layer brings new opportunities for users and developers to earn rewards by contributing resources or participating in AI ecosystems.

The collaboration between GAIB and KiteAI marks a significant milestone in the AI x Blockchain revolution. It highlights how decentralized compute can fuel the next generation of intelligent agents.
