Most people will be thinking: what is @gaib_ai really, and what role does it play inside @GoKiteAI?

Exploring GAIB’s role in powering compute and yield inside KiteAI.

Crypto_King877 · 5 min read

@gaib_ai is the economic layer for AI compute. It takes real-world GPU infrastructure and financing deals, then tokenizes them on-chain.

The result is AID (AI Dollar) — a synthetic dollar backed by GPU financing + U.S. T-bills.

By staking AID, users can earn passive yields, yet keep liquidity to use across DeFi.

Why does this matter for @GoKiteAI? Because every AI agent relies on compute at different stages — training, fine-tuning, inference, reasoning. Without scalable and cost-efficient compute, agents cannot be deployed or optimized at scale.

  • Making GPU-backed assets investable and yield-generating.
  • Providing a global network of cloud service providers across 10+ countries.
  • Allowing @GoKiteAI users to access compute while also investing directly in the infrastructure behind it.

So the connection is simple:
👉 @gaib_ai — supplies the compute + yield layer
👉 @GoKiteAI — provides the rails for agents (identity, payments, governance)
