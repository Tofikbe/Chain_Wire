Exploring GAIB’s role in powering compute and yield inside KiteAI.

@gaib_ai is the economic layer for AI compute. It takes real-world GPU infrastructure and financing deals, then tokenizes them on-chain.

The result is AID (AI Dollar) — a synthetic dollar backed by GPU financing + U.S. T-bills.

By staking AID, users can earn passive yields, yet keep liquidity to use across DeFi.
