KiteAI Roadmap – From Ozone Phase to Lunar

The KiteAI team has been moving at lightning speed. Just a few months ago, during the Ozone phase, the project focused on building foundational infrastructure and testing agent frameworks.

Today, KiteAI has already scaled towards the Lunar phase, onboarding real-time data partners and enhancing multi-agent collaboration. The growth trajectory has been exponential, with new ecosystems, alliances, and features being added every quarter.

From its early beginnings to where it stands today, KiteAI is redefining how we think about decentralized AI agents and their role in shaping the future of the internet.
